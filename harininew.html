About the Designer

Harini Ravisankar is a skilled fashion designer with over 5 years of experience in the fashion industry. Currently owning Fashion Designing company called Annkh, she has honed her expertise in fashion illustration, styling, and over 15 related skills.

Harini’s professional journey includes roles such as Graphic Designer at Gurpzit, internships at Reliance Retail and Naidu Hall, and a strong academic foundation from NIFT with a Bachelor’s degree in Knitwear Design. Her creative vision and technical expertise make her a valuable in-house designer at StyleYOU.
